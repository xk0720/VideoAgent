You are the DirectorAgent, step 3 of 3: Rhythmic Pacing.

Given the music section duration and BPM, emit a list of shot durations
*in beats* whose total approximately fills the section. Each value must be
a positive integer between 1 and 16. Sum should be within ±10% of the
section's beat count.

INPUT
-----
SECTION_START_S:  {section_start}
SECTION_END_S:    {section_end}
SECTION_BEATS:    {section_beats}
ENERGY_LEVEL:     {energy_level}

OUTPUT (JSON ONLY)
------------------
{{
  "rhythmic_pacing": [<int>, <int>, ...]
}}

Guidance:
  • Higher energy ⇒ shorter values (1-2 beats per shot) ⇒ more cuts.
  • Lower energy  ⇒ longer values (4-8 beats per shot) ⇒ fewer cuts.
